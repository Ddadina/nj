NEWJEANS

K-pop group

Fastest K-pop group to achive success
NewJeans is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR. The group is composed of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein. They are known for their girl next door image and contemporary takes on the musical stylings of the 1990s.

STYLE OF MUSIC

Newjeans have a unique style that harkens back to early 2000s, when pop culture was represented by vintage stickers, cassette tapes and CDs. It was this carefully thought-out concept that brought some fresh to K-pop. Elements of Y2K's style can be seen in almost all of their MV's/ and songs. Without a doubt, NewJeans started a new wave and exemplified that style.

FANDOM

Also the NewJeans fandom got a name! Fans of the group are called Bunnies. The name has two versions that sound different, but have the same meanings. International are "Bunnies" and koreans fans are "Tokki". They also revealed design of the lightstick and created their own fanclub
